Health

Health per Level: 8 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d10 per Level

Focused Flurry:

When you choose this class, and attack a target, you may make a second attack as part of the same action. When your level reaches 5, you can make two additional attacks, at level 10, you can make three additional attacks, and when you reach 15th level, you can make four additional attacks.

Battle Momentum:

When you attack a creature and drop it to 0 health, you can take another movement action or disengage. If you end the same turn within range if another creature, you can take another attack action.

Fighting Style:

Also when you choose this class, you get any Fighting Style type trait of your choice, without spending any points. To get any Fighting Style Trait, you must first have the base Trait.

1st Level Traits

Almighty Impel (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

When you make an attack you can choose to instead push the target a number of meters equal to your attack roll instead of dealing damage. If this knocks them into a wall they take 1d10 + Power score in Physical damage. If you launch them upwards, they take fall damage.

Charging Strike (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

If you spend your Movement Action this turn to move as far back as you can and then your next Movement Action to move directly forward, if a creature is within 1 meter (3 ft) when you end your second movement, you can add a +10 to the attack and knock them back 2 meters.

Defensive Stance (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action you can enter or exit Defense Stance. While in it, you gain an additional reaction that can be used only to increase your Armor by 3 when targeted by an attack, but your speed is halved, and you have a -2 disadvantage on attack rolls.

Duelist (Cost 1):

Type: Fighting Style

As a Bonus Action, you can feint an attack by rolling a Dexterity check against the targets Mind score. On a success, your next attack has a +3 advantage. Additionally, someone attacks you and misses you can immediately use your Reaction to make an attack against the creature that missed.

Additionally, when someone attacks you and misses can immediately use your Reaction to make an attack against the creature that missed.

Duelist Art: Powerful Thrust (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You must have the Duelist Trait. As a Bonus Action you can activate Powerful Thrust. On your next attack, you have an additional 1 meter (3 ft) of range and add 1d4 to the attack.

Grappler (Cost 1):

Type: Fighting Style

Your movement speed is quartered. You gain advantage on Grab rolls. Toss now deals damage equal to your Power Score.

Additionally, you gain a Double Action called Titanic Piledriver. Must have target grappled. This attack deals 5d10 + Power score in bludgeoning damage and ignores Armor.

Power Surge (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

You gain expend your Power Surge to take an additionally action of any kind, including allowing a second Main Action.

Pugilism (Cost 2):

Type: Fighting Style

You can make an additional two unarmed attacks as a Bonus Action, if your Main Action used exclusively unarmed attacks.

Additionally, your unarmed attacks do an additional 1d4 Physical damage.

Ranger (Cost 1):

Type: Fighting Style

If you're more than 15 meters (49 ft) away, you have a +2 advantage on ranged attacks.

When you make an attack that uses ammo, you can spend multiple ammo to do an Area Attack in a square equal to 1 meter (6 ft) per ammo spent, and cause a number of people equal to the ammo spent to roll a Dexterity check with a Difficulty of 12 + the amount spent.

Ranger Art: Deft Step (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You must have the Ranger Trait. You ignore natural terrain, don't need to roll to climb, and can climb at normal speed. You also gain a +2 advantage to Stealth while in natural terrain.

Skirmishing (Cost 1):

Type: Fighting Style

Your Health per Level decreases by 4. You have a 3rd action on your turn. It can only be used for an additional Movement Action. Your Speed doubles.

Additionally, you add a +1 to attack, and each consecutive attack that connects on your turn adds a +1 to attack.

Special Arts (Cost 1):

Type: Fighting Style

As an attack, you can release a blast of wind or energy from your fists or weapon. This projectile deals damage equal to the weapon and has a range of 5 meters (16 ft).

Additionally, you have an additional Reaction that can be used only to make attacks.

Spell Counter (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction to a non-physical Trait being used within your attack range, you can cancel that Trait from activating.

Undying Will (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You can spend a Health Die to remove one status effect from yourself.

Weapon Basics (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You gain a +2 to attacks with all weapons.

6th Level Traits

Duelist Art: Perfect Parry (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

You must have the Duelist Trait. As a Reaction when someone hits you with an attack, you can add +2 to your Armor. If this causes the attack to miss, you can immediately make an attack, adding 1d8 to the attack.

Soldier On! (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You have a +5 advantage to Constitution checks made to resist non-magical effects.

Grappler Art: Python Grip (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

You must have the Grappler Trait. When you Grab a creature, you can replace an attack with a Crush. This deals 2d8 + Power die in bludgeoning damage and ignores Armor. Using this causes you to drop the creature.

Additionally, you gain a new Reaction called Reversal. If a creature tries to escape your Grab, you can roll your Power die and add it to the Difficulty of the roll.

Indomitable Resolve (Cost 1):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per Encounter when you fail a roll, you may choose to completely reroll that roll.

Pugilism Arts: Martial Arts (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You may replace an attack with a leg sweep, Knocking the target but not dealing damage. Additionally, as a Main Action, you can do a Power Punch. You add your Level to the attack, and if you roll a Critical Attack on the Power Punch, you stun the creature for 10 seconds.

Pugilism Art: Rolling Thunder (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You must have the Pugilism Trait. As a Bonus Action, you can move half your speed without provoking attacks and allowing you to pass through the space of other creatures. Additionally, when you take damage from an unarmed attack, you can decrease the roll before it hits by a roll of your Constitution or Dexterity die.

Ranger Art: Golden Eye (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action, you can mark a creature as your Target. While they are your Target, you have a +5 advantage on Perception checks to find them, and add an additional weapon die to ranged attacks against them. Additionally, you cannot have any disadvantage posed on your Perception checks and can identify creatures at double the range a normal creature could.

Skirmishing Arts: Acrobat (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you Jump, you may Jump a second time in the air as part of the same action. Additionally, you have a +2 advantage on Acrobatics checks.

Skirmishing Arts: Evasive Assault (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

When you make an attack, you can move up to 10 feet immediately before or after the attack without provoking opportunity attacks. If you move before the attack, the target has disadvantage on their first attack against you until the start of your next turn. If you move after the attack, you can impose disadvantage on the first opportunity attack made against you before your next turn.

Special Arts: Flying Uppercut (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You must have the Special Arts Trait. As a Bonus Action, you can use a Flying Uppercut that deals 2d8 + Power in damage of your weapon's type. It has a range of 5 meters (16 ft) in the air and 1 meter (3 ft) also launches the target 5 meters (16 ft) into the air if they are grounded, or 3 meters (9 ft) away from you if they are airborne. It causes creatures in flight to be knocked down to the ground. It can also be used as a Reaction when a creature enters or leaves your range. This counts as an attack when used as a Reaction.

Unwavering Precision (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

Your Critical Hit range expands by one, as long as you roll any combination of the 2 highest numbers on your die, your attack counts as a Critical Hit.

Weapon Proficiency (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You must have the Weapon Basics Trait. You gain a +2 to attacks with all weapons.

13th Level Traits

Stone Cold Crazy (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You give a creature within sight a +4 advantage against you as a Bonus Action. If that creature hits you, you can automatically take a full attack action targeting that creature without using a Reaction.

Duelist Art: Focus Strike (Cost 2):

Type: Double Action

You must have the Duelist Trait. As a Double Action, you can make a single attack, adding 4d6 + your Mind Score to the attack.

Breaking the Law (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You may use a Bonus Action, and if you do, the next attack you make decreases the target's Armor by 2, whether or not it hits.

Grappler Art: Body Slam (Cost 2):

Type: Free Action

You must have the Grappler Trait. When you use a Jump as your Movement Action, you can choose a creature within the range of your Jump to land on, making an attack for 2d10 + your Power die as a Free Action.

The Last Stand (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction, when you are dropped to 0 Health and have no Health Die, you can return to 1 Health instead and immediately take a turn with a +5 advantage on all rolls during the rest of the round. Can be used once per rest.

Pugilism Art: Grab Break (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

You must have the Pugilism Trait. As a Reaction to being Grabbed, you may immediately make an unarmed attack. If it hits, you are not Grabbed, and the Grabber is unable to use the Grab action for 1 minute or until they heal at least 5 Health.

Ranger Art: Piercing Shot (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You must have the Ranger Trait. When you fire a projectile, you may choose another target within 1 meter (3 ft) to also take the damage. You may then choose another target within 5 feet (5 ft) of that creature that isn't the original target. You may repeat this process until there is no other creature you can hit.

Skirmishing Art: Throwing Stars (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You can throw a thrown weapon as part of a Movement Action.

Special Arts: Heavy Dash (Cost 2):

Type: Movement Action

You must have the Special Arts Trait. You can move up to half your Movement as a Movement Action, and any creature within 1 meter (3 ft) of the path you took takes Explosive damage equal to your Dexterity die + your Power die. You can Grab a creature within 1 meter (3 ft) at the end of your Movement if able.

Weapon Mastery (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You must have the Weapon Proficiency Trait. You gain a +2 to attacks with all weapons.

17th Level Traits

Grappler Art: Boundless Piledriver (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Double Action, you can perform the Boundless Piledriver on a creature you have Grabbed, a creature within 1 meter (3 ft) around you, or 3 meters (9 ft) into the air above you. You then deal 10d10 + five of your Power Die in Explosive damage and jump 10 meters (33 ft) into the air. You can choose to drop the creature there or land with them. You don't take fall damage either way.

Special Arts: Augmented Power (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Bonus Action, Once per Encounter, you can choose one of the following Augmented Power styles, which remain for 30 seconds:

  • Power of Absolute Offense: Add 1d8 Explosive damage to your attacks, and you can make one additional attack when you use the attack action.
  • Power of Undying Defense: Add a +2 to your Armor, you can't be moved against your will, and when you take damage that would drop you to 0 Health, you stay at 1 Health until this ends.
  • Power of True Speed: Your movement speed is tripled, and you no longer need to target only one creature when you use Focused Flurry.

True Power (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per Encounter, you can choose for an attack to ignore Armor and Resistances. It is automatically a Critical Attack.

I Will Give You My All (Cost 5):

Type: Double Action

For the next 4 minutes you are effectively immortal. Any time you take damage that is less than your maximum health, you immediately regenerate it, taking effectively no damage, and upon activation. Also your speed is tripled.
