Telekenesis:

When you choose this class, you gain the following options to use on creatures and objects in kilograms equal to 5 times your Mind Score:

Shunt: You can push a target up 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction, or pull them towards you the same distance. Grasp: You grab a target holding it in place until your next turn. It is unable to use actions, Launch: You launch a target up to 30 meters (99 ft) the air. They can be used in Reaction to an attack or creature entering your range targeting the creature that activated it, replacing an attack on your turn, or as a Bonus Action. You may also move items within your weight range freely, not requiring any kind of action. Additionally you can attack creatures even if they are Incorporeal.

Battle Momentum:

When you attack a creature and drop it to 0 health, you can take another movement action or disengage. If you end the same turn within range if another creature, you can take another attack action.

Fighting Style:

Also when you choose this class, you get any Fighting Style type trait of your choice, without spending any points. To get any Fighting Style Trait, you must first have the base Trait.
