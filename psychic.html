Health

Health per Level: 8 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d8 per Level

Telekenesis:

When you choose this class, you gain the following options to use on creatures and objects in kilograms equal to 5 times your Mind Score:

  • Shunt:You can push a target up 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction, or pull them towards you the same distance.
  • Grasp: You grab a target holding it in place until your next turn. It is unable to use actions.
  • Launch: You launch a target up to 30 meters (99 ft) the air.

    • They can be used in Reaction to an attack or creature entering your range targeting the creature that activated it, replacing an attack on your turn, or as a Bonus Action. You may also move items within your weight range freely, not requiring any kind of action. Additionally you can attack creatures even if they are Incorporeal.

    Psyche Burst:

    As an action or in reaction to an attack, all creatures within 10 meters must make a Constitution check against your Mind score are pushed back 2 meters (6 ft). Whether or not they succeed they also take 3d6 + Mind in psychic damage. You also take the same Psychic damage if its used as a Reaction.

    1st Level Traits

    Empathic Feedback (Cost 1):

    Type: Passive

    Whenever you are attacked, if the attack hits, the attacker takes damage equal to your Mind modifier.
