Tactician

Home Classes Origins Items

Health

Health per Level: 8 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d6 per Level

Tactical Advantage:

When you choose this class, when you land an attack on a creature, allies have a +2 advantage on attacks against that creature. The benefit changes when you attack another creature.

Strategic Command:

As a Bonus Action you can command one willing creature to take either a Movement or Main Action.

1st Level Traits

Acute Awareness (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

As a Main Action, you can learn the location of all invisible creatures within 10 meters (33 ft).

Battlefield Adaptation (Cost 1):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Free Action at the start of your turn, once per encounter, you can add +5 to one attack roll, skill check, or check.

Cover Fire (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You can choose an ally within 10 meters (33 ft) to gain a +2 to Armor.

Enemy Analysis (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action, you can roll an Insight check. If you roll higher than their Aura score, you can learn one Attribute of that target.

Inspiring Leader (Cost 1):

Type: Double Action

You can choose any number of creatures within 10 meters (33 ft) to gain Health equal to your level and remove the Fear condition.

Move It (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You and an ally within sight can move up to half of your speed.

Strategic Insight (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You have a +2 advantage on Perception and Insight checks during combat.

Quick Thinking (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction, you can allow an ally to add +2 to their next non-attack roll.

Team Coordination (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you use a Bonus Action ability that affects an ally, you can choose another ally within 30 ft to gain a +2 advantage on their next roll.

Withdrawal (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you use the Disengage action, you can choose another ally within 5 meters (16 ft) to also Disengage, and you gain a +2 to your Armor.

6th Level Traits

Action Surge (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction when a creature lands a Critical Attack, you can immediately allow them to make another attack as a Free Action.

Chain of Command (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

Once per turn, when you use a Tactician Trait that takes a Bonus Action, you can activate a different Tactician Trait that also uses a Bonus Action as a Free Action.

Counter-Strategy (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature targets an ally with an effect that requires a check, you can give them a +1 advantage as a Reaction. If a creature targets an ally with an attack, you can grant them a +1 to Armor. The target of this effect must be within 10 meters (33 ft).

Rush (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Double Action, you and another ally can move up to half their speed. Then you can immediately use a Bonus Action, and the ally can immediately use a Main Action.

Position Analysis (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature within 10 meters (33 ft) of you moves, you can use a Reaction to move up to half your speed. During this movement, you cannot be attacked.

Rallying Call (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

You can grant all allies within a 15 meter (50 ft) radius the Inspired Status Effect. This gives them a +2 advantage on attacks and Constitution checks.

Survival Tactics (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you use an ability that targets an ally and has a limited range, if an ally is within range, the range extends equal to the ability's original range from that ally.

13th Level Traits

Epitaph (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You can choose any dead creature, and use your Bonus Action to learn either 2 of its stats, its health and 1 stat, its Armor and 1 stat, or its Armor and Health.

Vulgar Display of Power (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You may choose a creature within 10 meters (33 ft) to make an immediate attack against an enemy within range, granting them the ability to reroll the attack if they miss. If the attack hits, it deals additional damage equal to your Intelligence modifier.

Duck (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You give any creatures of your choice within a 10 meter (33 ft) range a +3 advantage on Dexterity checks.

Perfect Remedy (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Main Action, you can choose up to two creatures within 10 meters (33 ft) and grant them the ability to reroll their next failed saving throw within one minute.

Tactical Retreat (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

When you or an ally within 30 feet takes damage, you can use your reaction to allow that individual to immediately move up to their speed without provoking opportunity attacks. Additionally, they gain temporary hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier until the start of your next turn.

17th Level Traits

Advanced Observation (Cost 3):

Type: Main Action

Quick Strategy (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

You may designate a number of allies equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum one) within 10 meters (33 ft) to gain a one-time bonus to their next attack roll equal to your Intelligence modifier. You also gain a +2 advantage on attack rolls against the targets of their attacks.

On Your Feet (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

When you or an ally within 30 feet is reduced to 0 hit points, you can use your reaction to instead reduce them to 1 hit point and immediately allow them to move up to half their movement speed and take the Dodge action. Additionally, they gain temporary hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier + your level.
