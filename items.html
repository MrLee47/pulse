Armor Classes

Light Armor (Armor 10)

You have a -1 disadvantage on Acrobatics checks.

Medium Armor (Armor 12)

You have a -2 disadvantage on Acrobatics checks and your speed is decreased by 5.

Heavy Armor (Armor 14)

You have a -4 disadvantage on Acrobatics checks and your speed is decreased by 10. Whenever you are moved against your will, you may halve the distance moved.

Flexible Armor (Armor 2 + Dexterity Score)

The Armor minimizes noise, giving everyone a -2 disadvantage on finding you based on sound.

Mega Armor (Armor 10 + Constitution Score)

Your speed is halved. You have a +2 advantage on Athletics checks. You have a -10 disadvantage on Acrobatics checks. Your Physical Resistance is doubled while wearing this Armor.

Weapons

Baton (1d8 + Power or Dexterity Die)

When you miss with an attack you can use your Bonus Action to deal half damage anyway, or to Knock the target.

Bow (1d6 + Dexterity Die)

As a Bonus Action before you attack, you can spend one action to load up to your Dexterity Score in arrows. When you attack, you can choose to fire any number of loaded arrows, making a separate attack roll for each.

Broadsword (2d4 + Power Die)

If you roll a Critical Hit, the target is knocked 3 meters (9 ft) back and is Stunned.

Chakram (1d8 + Dexterity Die)

When thrown, if you roll higher than a 4 on the weapon die, you deal the Dexterity Die damage even if you didn't hit. If you deal damage with the Chakram, you can then choose another target within 2 meters of the original target to be hit, but you cannot return to someone you've already damaged this turn.

Dagger (1d4 + Power or Dexterity Die)

You may make an attack with a Dagger as part of a Movement Action.

Firearm (1d10 + Dexterity Die)

You can use your Mind to attack instead of Dexterity, and when you roll a Critical Hit, it deals times 4 damage instead of times 2.

Greataxe (3d4 + Power Die)

If you reduce a creature to 0 health with a Greataxe, you can make a melee attack against another creature within 1 meter as a Free Action."

Greatsword (2d8 + Power Die)

While wielding a Greatsword, your Armor is increased by one.

Halberd (2d6 + Power Die)

If you hit with an attack with this weapon, you can deal 1d4 damage to another target within 1 meter as a Free Action.

Katars (1d8 + Power Die)

If you miss, you gain a +2 Armor. If you hit with Katars during your Main Action, you can use your Bonus Action to make one additional attack with them for each attack you made during your Main Action.

Longsword (1d10 + Power or Dexterity Die)

If you miss, you gain a +1 to your next attack.

Macuahuitl (2d4 + Power Die)

Once per turn you can increase the weapons Critical Range by 1, for one attack.

Maul (2d10 + Power Die)

On a Critical Hit everyone in a 3 meter radius is Knocked.

Pike (1d10 + Power Die)

When you roll a Critical Hit, the opponent is immediately Grabbed.

Quarterstaff (1d8 + Dexterity or Power Die)

You can make an attack with this weapon once as a Bonus Action, and you must use the opposite stat, and if it hits the opponent is Knocked.

Scythe (1d10 + Dexterity or Power Die)

If you reduce a creature to 0 health with the Scythe you regain a Health Die.

Spade (1d8 + Dexterity or Power)

Once per turn, whether or not you hit, when you attack you may choose to lower the opponents Armor by 1 until the end of your next turn.

Spear (1d8 + Power)

If you make a ranged attack with the Spear, you can choose to push the target back 1 meter (3 ft) on a hit.

Urumi (3d4 + Power)

If you make an attack with the Urumi you can make the weapon die split into different attacks that ignore armor, as long as the first attack hits. For example, 2 targets take 1d4 and the other 2d4, or 3 take 1d4.

Whip

If you hit a creature with a Whip, you can choose to reduce their movement speed by half until the end of their next turn or grapple them as a Bonus Action.
