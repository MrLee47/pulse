Secret Origins

Home Classes Origins Items

These Secret Origins are selectable upon character creation, and one may be chosen for free when selecting origin features. These cannot be gained any other way.

Hyper Attack:

Upon using an action you may use your next action this turn to immediately repeat that same action. This action is carried out the same way, against the same target, unless it included movement, in which case you may move in any direction but may not change targets still if there is an attack, also doesn't cost any price to use the action an additional time, and ignores cooldown and use limits, but cannot be used on a Healing Surge. You only have one action on your next 2 turns.

Quick Dash:

You can double your speed as a free action. You cannot do this again until you have spent a turn without moving.

Thinker:

You gain four additional points that can only be used to increase Skill proficiencies.
