Health

Health per Level: 6 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d6 per Level

Sharp Stealth:

When you choose this class, you can hide as part of any movement action. Additionally, when you deal damage with a weapon you can immediately take another Movement Action. You may only use this up to twice on your turn, or up to 5 times at level 15.

Ghostly Dash:

As a reaction to being attacked, you can add your Dexterity modifier to your Armor. If this allows you to dodge the attack, you can move 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction, and your Speed is doubled. Additionally, you can no longer be attacked on your turn if you move in or out of a creatures range, and if you use a Movement Action on your turn your Armor increases by 2 until the start of your next turn.

1st Level Traits

Ambush Tactics (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You have a +5 advantage to attack targets that haven't gone in combat yet.

Expertise (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

Choose two different Skills. Add a +2 to your proficiency with them. The maximum for these skills increases to +8.

National Acrobat (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can run up walls when you use a Movement Action, but you can't remain on a wall for more than a round.

Points of Authority (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you deal damage with a Dagger, add +2 to the damage dealt.

Go With the Flow (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You can automatically escape from a Grab or other non-magical shackles. You cannot automatically escape the Grab of a person with the Grappler Fighting Style.

Shadow Strike (Cost 2):

Type: Movement Action

If you are Hiding, you can move 5 meters (16 ft) and make an attack against a creature within range, adding 1d6 damage to the attack. If you have Sharp Stealth, you can move your full speed when using this.

Talk to the Wind (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can communicate simple messages and intentions to nearby allies without speaking or making any obvious movements.

Silent Takedown (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

While you have a creature Grabbed, you can use a Bonus Action to incapacitate the creature as long as its Constitution is less than your Power.

Last Caress (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you deal damage to a creature with a Sword or Dagger weapon, the creature takes an additional die of damage at the start of its turn.

Tactical Thinking (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You can now Disengage, Dodge, or Help as a Bonus Action rather than a Main Action.

6th Level Traits

No Surprises (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can't be Ambushed, gain a +3 advantage on all Perception and Insight checks, and have a +3 advantage on attacks made as a Reaction.

Blindsided (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you attack a creature who currently has a Status Effect, you can add your level to the attack.

Nimble Reflex (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

If you fail a check to resist damage from an effect, you only take half the damage.

Quick Fingered (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You have a +3 advantage on checks made to disarm a trap or open a lock.

Speed Blitz (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you attack a creature that's slower than you, you can roll your Power or Dexterity die and add it to the attack.

Never Meant to Know (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You can move up to half your speed completely silently, and Perception checks to find you have a -5 disadvantage.

Careless Whisper (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You can use your Bonus Action to make your next attack silently. If it kills the creature, your position isn't revealed. Otherwise, your position is still revealed, but you can immediately take another Hide action.

13th Level Traits

Bait and Switch (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

Using your Movement Action, you can give a creature within sight a -2 disadvantage on their next attack.

Disarm Reflex (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

When a creature hits you with an attack, you can use your Bonus Action to Disarm them if they are within range. You can roll either a Power or Dexterity check against their Power score, and on a success, you can take their weapon and may immediately throw it as part of the same Reaction or keep it.

Gadgeteer (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You can use items as a Bonus Action rather than a Main Action.

Mind Electric (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You can target a creature within 30 feet that can see and hear you. The creature must make a Wisdom saving throw against your Dexterity score. On a failed save, the creature is charmed by you for 1 minute or until it takes damage.

Circadian Rhythms (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

If you roll lower than your Mind Score on a skill, you can use your Mind Score for the roll instead.

17th Level Traits

Nerve Strike (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

You can make a melee attack against a creature. On a hit, the creature must make a Constitution check against your Dexterity Score. On a failed save, the creature is stunned for 30 seconds.

Grand Heist (Cost 3):

Type: Bonus Action

You can instantly steal a non-magical item from a creature within 30 feet without them noticing. The item must be something they could reasonably have on their person. This ability can be used once per long rest.

One with the Shadows (Cost 3):

Type: Passive

You can remain hidden even when you take an action to move or attack. You can reveal yourself as a free action at any time, to specific creatures only if you wish.
