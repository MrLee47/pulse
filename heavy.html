Health

Health per Level: 15 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d4 per Level

Charged Die:

You can use a Bonus Action to increase the weapon die value of your next attack by one. When you use this, you can move up to 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction or use a Jump. At level 10, your die value increase by 2 rather than 1. If the die value would exceed a d12, add an additional 1d4 and increase that dies value if it increases beyond that.

Overpowering Resilience::

As a Bonus Action, you can activate Overpowering Resilience, providing yourself with an additional layer of "Soak" that absorbs damage for 1 minute. During this time, you gain additional Health equal to 3 times the difference between your Maximum Health and your current Health. Once the duration ends or you've taken that much damage, the additional Health is gone and cannot be regained. This does not Heal any of your missing Health or count as a healing effect. This can be used 3 times during an Encounter, and can also be used as a Reaction to being attacked.

1st Level Traits

Bounding Leap (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can multiply your Jump distance, vertical or long jump, by a roll of your Constitution die each time you Jump.

Coconut Catch (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature within 10 meters (33 ft) of you is falling, you can use your reaction to Grab it, forcing you to take the fall damage instead.

Ground Shatter (Cost 1):

Type: Double Action

You can perform a quick leap, choosing an area within 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction and landing, dealing 3d10 Physical damage in a 10 meter (33 ft) area, damaging all creatures in that range.

Hardened Strikes (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can add your Constitution die to your attacks if you currently have Soak.

Eagle Snatch (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature misses an attack against you, you can attempt to Grab the creature as a Reaction.

Repositioning Move (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature you see attacks a target other than you that is within 5 meters (16 ft), you can use your reaction to swap positions with the target creature. You become the new target of the attack.

Death Grip (Cost 1):

Type: Free Action

When you attack someone, you can Grab them as a Free Action.

Thunderous Blows (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can add 1d4 Explosive damage to your melee attacks.

Unkillable Behemoth (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you use Overpowering Resilience, your height and weight double..

Unyielding Blows (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

Your melee attacks deal an additional 1d4 Physical damage. Additionally, each attack reduces the target's speed by 10 meters until the start of your next turn.

6th Level Traits

Absent Minded (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You can remove the Stunned effect from yourself as a Bonus Action. Additionally, you take halved damage from critical attacks.

Hardcore Hustle (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You can attack as a Reaction during your turn, but you must do it immediately after your Main Action. This Reaction can only be one attack.

Beastly Regeneration (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You heal twice as much from all sources, including Health Die.

Defensive Positioning (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action, you become immune to any effect that could slow you down, such as a Trait or specific terrain until the start of your next turn.

Mighty Constitution (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

When you roll a Constitution check or skill, you can use your Constitution Score instead.

Righteous Mind (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You gain an additional Reaction if you don't attack on your turn.

Crazy, Mad, Insane (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You halve all damage taken during Overpowering Resilience.

13th Level Traits

Hands of Doom (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You unleash a devastating strike. Make a melee attack that deals 8d10 Physical damage. If the attack hits, all creatures within a 5-meter (16 ft) radius of the target take 3d10 Physical damage.

Colossal Charge (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You can attempt a powerful charge at a target within 15 meters (50 ft). If you reach the target, make a melee attack that deals 6d10 Physical damage and pushes the target 5 meters (16 ft) away from you. On a hit, you can also choose to knock the target prone.

Earthshattering Stomp (Cost 2):

Type: Double Action

You stomp the ground forcefully, creating a shockwave. All creatures within a 10-meter (33 ft) radius must make a Strength saving throw. On a failure, they take 4d8 Physical damage and are knocked prone. On a success, they take half damage and are not knocked prone.

Dead End (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction to an attack you can increase your Armor by 4 until the end of the enemies turn. If the attack still hits you gain resistance to that kind of damage until the start of your next turn.

Indomitable Will (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

When you are hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to roll a Constitution saving throw against the attack. On a success, you fully negate the damage and gain health equal to the damage taken.

17th Level Traits

The Wall (Cost 3):

Type: Passive

Your Maximum Health increase by 10 times your Consitution Score.

Shine on You Crazy Diamond (Cost 5):

Type: Double Action

You make a single melee attack. You lose all Health granted by Overpowering Resilience. Add 1d10 Explosive damage per 5 Health lost to this attack. You also take 5d10 Physical damage. If this kills you, you may choose a target to gain health equal to the amount of Health Die you have as Soak, it doesn't go away until they've taken that damage.

Dark Side of the Moon (Cost 4):

Type: Double Action

You unleash a catastrophic attack that shakes the very ground beneath you. Choose a point within 30 meters (99 ft). All creatures within a 10-meter (33 ft) radius take 10d10 Physical damage. Additionally, the ground causes all enemies speed to be halved for 1 minute, and they are knocked prone.
