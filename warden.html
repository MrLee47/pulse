Warden

Health

Health per Level: 6 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d10 per Level

Cosmic Tether:

When you choose this class you can tether a number of creatures equal to your Constitution Score. You may choose to tether either allies or enemies as a Bonus Action or Reaction. When you tether you and/or allies together, if any ally is healed or damaged while tethered it splits the total healing or damage among the group. Additionally upon activation, tethered allies become Inspired and can roll 1d10 and add it to their health at the start of their turns. If you tether enemies, they gain the Slowed effect, and all damage dealt to any target is also dealt to all other tethered enemies. Upon activation it lasts until your next turn, and you have 2 charges, with each charge recharging after 50 seconds.

Lifeweaver:

You can use your Health Die to heal any creature within 10 meters (33 ft) of you as a Bonus Action. When you do so, you gain half they health they do rounded down.

1st Level Traits

Blinding Radiance (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature tries to hit you with an attack you may release a snap of blinding light, as you and up to 4 allies within 3 meters (9 ft) count as Invisible until the start of your next turn.

Cursed Body (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

When a creature hits you with a Trait you can roll a 1d4, and on a 4 that trait is disabled for a minute. On anything lower than a 4, you regain your reaction.

Eruption (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

Anyone within 10 meters (33 ft) makes a Consitution check against your Mind, and on a failure takes 5d10 Explosive damage. On a success they take half as much.

Eye of Radiance (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

Anyone within 20 meters (66 ft) makes a Consitution check against your Mind, and on a failure takes 4d10 Divine damage. On a success they take half as much. For 20 seconds after you activate this, allies recieve twice the healing from all Warden traits.

Godspeed Emperors (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per encounter, you can grant yourself and up to 5 allies an additional action. You may still only use one Main Action on your turn.

Guiding Light (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

Grant allies a +2 advantage on a target of your choice.

Quick Recovery (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action you can recover a Health Die.

Resilient Soul (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You gain an additional Health Die per level.

Supreme Enhancements (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction when an ally makes a roll of any kind you can increase one of their stats by one die. If Cosmic Tether is active, this applies the effect to all allies until the start of your next turn.

Tethered Positioning (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you move while Cosmic Tether is active on you and at least one other creature, all other tethered creatures may move up to half the Speed you did.

6th Level Traits

Astral Shunt (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

When you make an attack you can shunt the targets soul out of its body, Stunning it until the start of your next turn. It takes double damage from area attacks as they target the soul and boy.

Golden Barrier (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action you can coat yourself in a shimmering barrier, and the first attack that targets you and hits takes 2d8 Force damage, ignoring armor.

Force Blast (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

Choose a target within range and make an attack that deals 2d10 + Mind die Force damage.

Interstellar Shift (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

Whenever you are hit by an attack, you can halve the damage as a Reaction as you enter the Spiritual Realm, and then you gain free movement in all dimensions and can reposition yourself anywhere in a 15 meter (49 ft) radius around your body.

Weakening Pulse (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

Choose one enemy within 10 meters (33 ft). Until the start of your next turn, that enemy has a -2 disadvantage on Strength and Dexterity checks.

Shooting Blanks (Cost 2):

When a tethered enemy within range uses a Trait ability, you can use your Reaction to force them to make a Mind check against your Mind score. If they don't succeed, the Trait has no effect.

Vulnerabilty Link (Cost 2):

If Cosmic Tether is active on at least two enemies, you can choose one of those enemies. Until the start of your next turn, that enemy takes double damage from the next attack they take.

13th Level Traits

Cosmic Nova (Cost 2):

Type: Double Action

You unleash a burst of cosmic energy in a 10 meter (33 ft) radius around you. All creatures in the area must make a Dexterity check against your Mind score. On a failure, they take 8d10 Force damage and are Knocked On a success, they take half damage and are not Knocked, but have their Speed decreased by 5 meters.

Atomic (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You unleash an explosion of force and power, dealing 2d10 Force and 2d10 Explosive damage to all targets within 10 meters (33 ft). If an enemy who is hit is tethered, you can end the tether at the end of the action to do an additional 3d10 Explosive and 1d10 Force to all tethered enemies.

Cosmic Harmony (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

If Cosmic Tether is active with allies, you can choose one stat. Until the start of your next turn, all tethered allies gain a +2 advantage on checks and saving throws using that stat.

Guardian Angel (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

Whenever you are dropped to 0 Health, you may turn into a spirit and move freely for 10 seconds, at the end of these 10 seconds, you reform with 10 Health. This recharges after 120 seconds, and cannot activate if you were killed with Divine, Void, or Force damage. Additionally, When a tethered ally within range is about to take a large amount of damage (more than half of their health), you can use your Reaction to take all of that damage yourself instead.

Draining Plain (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

Create a 5 meter (16 ft) radius disk centered on a point you can see within 20 meters (66 ft). For the next minute, any enemy that starts their turn in this disk has their movement speed halved and has a -2 disadvantage on attacks.

17th Level Traits

The Day I Tried to Live (Cost 5):

Type: Double Action

On your turn, whether or not you are alive, you can use a Double Action to ressurect all allies within 30 meters (98 ft) as well as yourself. They come back with half of their maximum health, rounded down.

Blow Up the Outside World (Cost 4):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Double Action you unleash a cataclysmic burst of cosmic energy. Choose a point within sight. All creatures within a 15 meter (49 ft) radius of that point take 10d10 Force damage and 10d10 Explosive damage. Tethered enemies within the area take an additional 5d10 Force damage. After using this ability, you are Stunned until the end of your next turn due to the sheer power unleashed. The blinding energy makes it so you and all allies count as Invisible for 10 seconds.

Burden in My Hand (Cost 4):

Type: Main Action

Choose one target within 15 meters (49 ft), and they have a -4 disadvantage on all checks and have their Speed, Armor, and Might halved. You can end this at any point as a Reaction, but while it is up, all the same effects happen to you. While Burden in My Hand is active, you and a number of allies up to your Mind Score gain 1d4 health at the start of each allies turn.
