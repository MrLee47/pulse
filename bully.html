Bully Class

Overview

The Bully class specializes in close combat and intimidation. Known for their brute strength and aggressive tactics, Bullies overpower their enemies with raw physical power and psychological dominance. They excel in brawling, grappling, and using their environment to their advantage.

Abilities

Bullies have a unique set of abilities that revolve around causing fear and maximizing damage in melee combat. They can intimidate opponents, reducing their effectiveness in battle, and possess powerful strikes that can knock back or stun enemies. Their resilience also makes them difficult to take down in a fight.

Playstyle

Playing as a Bully means taking the front lines and using your strength to control the battlefield. You'll want to get up close and personal with your enemies, using your intimidating presence to disrupt their plans and your brute force to bring them down.
