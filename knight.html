Health

Health per Level: 8 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d8 per Level

Elemental Infusion:

When you choose this class, you can add an additional die of Physical, Explosive, Divine, Psychic, Magic, or Void damage to the attack. This increases to two additional die at a total level of 12. You can only change the damage type when you level up.

Arcane Slash:

You can swing your weapon towards a target within 10 meters (33 ft) and shoot a projectile of your element. It only does the damage of your weapon that is of your element, which is the same as your Elemental Infusion element. This takes a Bonus Action.

1st Level Traits

Arcane Deflection (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a reaction, you can decrease damage taken from any source by 1d8 + your Mind modifier.

Binding Weapon (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You choose a weapon upon picking this ability; you can now magically stow or summon the weapon as a Bonus Action or Reaction, or recall it to your hand from anywhere with either a Reaction or Bonus Action. If summoned in an object, it appears within 5 ft in an open space.

Black Rainbows (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

You can jump up to 10 meters (33 ft) up, and any creature within 4 meters (12 ft) of the area you jumped takes 3d8 damage of the type of your Elemental Infusion, or physical damage if you don't have an Elemental Infusion.

Blink (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You can teleport up to 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction.

Colossal Strikes (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Double Action, you can make an attack, replacing your weapon's base die with 3d12 Force damage.

Eldritch Parry (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

As a reaction to an attack, you can add a +2 to your Armor.

Esoteric Pulse (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

Whenever you land a critical hit, creatures within 3 meters (9 ft) of the target take 2d8 Magic damage.

Justice Cleave (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Double Action, pick up to 5 targets; you dash between all of them dealing Magic damage equal to your weapon's dice. These attacks ignore Armor.

Rune Empowerment (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

As a bonus action, you may apply one of the following Runes to a weapon you're holding; it lasts for 1 minute. You may have multiple runes applied to the same weapon.

  • Rune of Flame: Upon hitting an attack, you add an additional 1d4 damage. Additionally, the target takes 1d4 of your Elemental Infusion damage type (or Explosive if you don't have one) at the start of each of their turns.
  • Rune of Frost: Each attack you hit with this weapons removes 5 meters (16 feet) of speed from the target until the beginning of your next turn.
  • Rune of Healing: As a Bonus Action you can use your Healing Die (up to half your Level at once) on a target within 10 meters (33 ft) other than yourself. You cannot raise a creature from 0 Health.
  • Rune of Protection: You and allies within 3 meters (9 ft) half damage from all non-magical sources.
  • Rune of Stone: If an attack with this weapon hits, you can choose to Knock the target or knock the target back 3 meters (9 ft).
  • Rune of Space: You can use a Bonus Action to pull a target within sight 5 meters (16 ft) towards you.

    • Splash Attack (Cost 1):

    Type: Main Action

    You shoot a 10 by 5 meter line of your Elemental Infusion, and all creatures must roll a Dexterity check equal to your Mind score. If they fail, they take 5d6 damage of your elemental type. If you don't have an Elemental Infusion, it deals physical damage and requires a Constitution check against your Power.

    6th Level Traits

    Arcane Recovery (Cost 2):

    Type: Once per Encounter

    As a Bonus Action Once per Encounter, you can restore one Once per Encounter Trait, allowing you to use it a second time.

    Divine Sense (Cost 1):

    Type: Bonus Action

    As a Bonus Action, you become alerted of all supernatural or magical entities within a 30 meter (99 ft) range. Additionally, you gain a +4 advantage to Insight when determining if someone is lying.

    Healing Hands (Cost 1):

    Type: Bonus Action

    As a Bonus Action, you can remove a single Status Effect from another creature you can touch. Roll your Mind die; if you roll above half, it succeeds. If you roll below half, nothing happens.

    Soul Sacrifice (Cost 2):

    Type: Bonus Action

    You can sacrifice 25 health as a Bonus Action to double the amount of die on the next attack you make.

    Tachyon (Cost 1):

    Type: Main Action

    As your Main Action, you can increase your speed and one ally's speed by 10 meters (33 ft).

    Wild Call (Cost 1):

    Type: Main Action

    You can summon a spectral animal that can lift up to 20 kilograms (45 lbs) and can be desummoned as a Bonus Action or Reaction. Choose one:

  • Fish: Has a speed of 10 meters, can swim.
  • Rat: Has a speed of 15 meters.
  • Bird: Has a speed of 5 meters, can fly.

    • Zippy Impact (Cost 1):

    Type: Movement Action

    As a Movement Action, you can form a spring. On your next Movement Action, you can move 5 times your speed. The first non-magical wall you hit is destroyed.

    13th Level Traits

    Elemental Guardian (Cost 1):

    Type: Bonus Action

    As a Bonus Action, you can create a small elemental (you can choose what element it appears as). As a Reaction, you can force the Elemental Guardian to take the damage of an attack or one negative effect from a Trait for you or an ally; then it disappears.

    Empowering Aura (Cost 2):

    Type: Passive

    Creatures in a 5 meter (16 ft) range take half damage from your Elemental Infusion type. They also add 1d6 of your Elemental Infusion damage type to melee attacks. If you don't have an Elemental Infusion, the damage type for both of these abilities is Physical.

    Flight of the Elementals (Cost 2):

    Type: Movement Action

    You can fly up to 10 meters (33 ft) off of the ground. At the end of your turn, you fall from the sky, ignoring fall damage.

    Resilient Bulwark (Cost 1):

    Type: Once per Encounter

    Once per Encounter, as your Main Action, you can double all Resistances for 30 seconds.

    Right in Two (Cost 2):

    Type: Double Action

    As a Double Action, you can touch a surface. A 5 meter (16 ft) radius cylinder blasts 20 meters (66 ft) outward from the surface you touched. Creatures in it must make a Constitution check. On a failure, they take 10d8 damage of your Elemental Infusion type. If you don't have an Elemental Infusion, the default damage type is Explosive.

    17th Level Traits

    Forests of Denial (Cost 3):

    Type: Once per Encounter

    Once per Encounter, using your Main Action, everyone you choose in a 30 meter (99 ft) radius of you heals 8d10 Health. For the next 30 seconds at the start of each of your turns you heal everyone an additional 4d10 Health. Additionally, all broken or lost limbs oe body parts are restored and fixed.

    Little Piece of Heaven (Cost 3):

    Type: Once per Encounter

    Choose an area within sight as a Main Action. Centering on that area, a 10 meter (33 ft) radius beam of light descends upon the land. Creatures inside take 12d8 Divine damage, ignoring Armor, and become Blinded for 30 seconds.

    Mystic Overload (Cost 3):

    Type: Once per Encounter

    As a Bonus Action Once per Encounter, you can double the damage of your next attack and increase its range by 5 meters (16 ft). Additionally, if it hits, the attack deals damage to all creatures within 5 meters (16 ft) of the original target.
