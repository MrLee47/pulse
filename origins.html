Origins

Home Classes Origins Items Prestige

Your characters Origins include their innate traits, things that are innate to your character before your Class, built in to your character since birth. Although many of the things can be improved, they are also intrisic to your character and apply to all characters.

Speed

Your speed is a number of meters equal to 5 times your Dexterity Score. If you are Large, decrease by 5. If you are Small, you may choose to add 25 to your speed, if you don't choose this, count your Might as if you were Medium, and Resistance as if you were Large.

Might

You can lift a number of kilograms equal to 50 times your Power Score times 2 if your character is Medium, times 5 if your character is Large, without effort. You can lift up 5 times that amount with rolls and at least some amount of struggle.

Shape

You can choose any height between 150 and 250 cm traditionally, but any is allowed. Your weight can also be anything, but is traditionally between 25 and 200 kg depending on height. There are 3 size brackets that help determine some stats and can settle certain conflicts in game.

Small - Less than 180 cm or under 64 kg (5'10 and 140 lbs) Medium - Less than 190 cm or under 100 kg (6'2 and 200 lbs) Large - Above 200 cm or above 120 kg (6'6 and 265 lbs)

There are some cases where you land between two size brackets, in which case you and your GM decide which is more fitting.

Resistances:

Your Origin grants you a number of points you can put into your Resistances equal three times your Constitution Score, plus 5 if you are Medium, plus 10 if you are Large. You can also gain Resistances from items, class features, and other things. You cannot have a higher resistance in a single type than your Constitution Score + your Level.
