Unique Trait

If you choose the Blood Prestige you become stronger the lower your Health is. When you are below Maximum Health, add 1 to all attacks. When you are below 75% of your health, you add 2 to all attacks, decrease your Armor by 1, and your range increases by 1 meter (3 ft). When you are below 50% health, add 4 to all attacks, decrease your Armor by 1, and you range increases by 2 meters. When you are below 25% health, add 5 to all attacks, decrease your Armor by 2, and your range increases by 3 meters. If you are below 10% health, add 10 to all attacks, decrease Armor your by 5, and your range increases by 10 meters. The moment you hit 0 Health, you can immediately take a turn. The range increase does not apply to the Unarmed Attack granted by Bloodsucking Universe.

1st Level Traits

Vampiric Reflex: (Cost 2):

If you successfully bite a target with your Bloodsucking Universe ability as part of your Main Action, you can choose to immediately take an additional action. This action can be a Main Action, but you can only gain this additional action once.

Vicious Counter: (Cost 1):

Whenever you are attacked during your turn before you use your Main Action, you can immediately use your Main Action to target the creature trying to attack you, cancelling their attack.
