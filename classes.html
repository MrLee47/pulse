Heavy

Heavies embody the essence of unyielding aggression, either smashing through barriers or laying waste to anything that dares confront them. The overpowering nature of their sheer physicality allows them to absorb devastating blows with little regard for their own safety.

Knight

Striking a unique balance between brute strength, tactical utility, and magical prowess, they are versatile warriors on the battlefield. While they possess the raw physicality of a melee combatant, Knights also draw from a well of divine, arcane, and primal magic to enhance their versatility, allowing them to adapt to dynamic combat situations.

Rogue

The Rogue specializes in stealth, speed, and precision. They strike from the shadows, exploiting their enemies’ weaknesses before vanishing without a trace. With unparalleled agility and a mastery of deception, the Rogue thrives in environments where surprise and quick thinking can turn the tide of battle in an instant.

Type: Flank

Ease of Play: ★★★★★

Sorcerer

A master of Magic who has mana flowing through its very blood. The Sorcerer uses powerful unarmed strikes and spells to overpower enemies while supporting themself and allies. Its wide array of techniques and spells draw from arcane magics and mana, and their powerful innate techniques can heavily increase characters power.

Ease of Play: ★☆☆☆☆

The Warden is a master of cosmic connections, wielding magical tethers to bolster allies and hinder foes. With the ability to mend wounds and manipulate energies, the Warden serves as the anchor for their party, ensuring their survival and orchestrating their victories through strategic links and timely support.

The Hunter is a swift and agile marksman, a humanoid typhoon who thrives on precision and speed on the battlefield. With the keen instincts of a predator, the Hunter excels at identifying weaknesses in enemy defenses and delivering ranged attacks that strike true and fast. This class darts in and out of cover to maintain the upper hand against foes while remaining elusive and difficult to pin down.
