Health

Health per Level: 6 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d10 per Level

Mana Manipulation:

When you choose this class, you can as a Bonus Action manipulate latent mana, adding a die equal to your Intelligence in Magic damage to your next Trait ability or unarmed attack. This increases to two die at level 8, and three die at level 16.

Perfect Impact:

When you roll a Critical Attack with an unarmed strike, Mana Manipulation is automatically applied for the next 20 seconds. Your Critical Range then expands by one. Getting another critical with an unarmed strike during this time resets the timer, and expands your Critical Range by 1 again, and it repeats until your attacks are Critical on all but a 1. Then, the timer resets on a Critical Attack, but your Critical Range no longer expands.

Innate Talent

:

You can choose one Sorcerous Talent trait for free upon picking this Class. You gain the Innate ability of this trait.

1st Level Traits

Arcane Gate (Cost 2):

Type: Sorcerous Talent

As a Bonus Action, you can create a set of 2 meter by 1 meter (6 x 3 ft) doors somewhere within sight. If someone walks through them, the doors automatically close on them, dealing 3d6 force damage. You can use a Reaction at any point to close them early, creating an impassable magic barrier, or use your Main Action to create them around a target, immediately dealing the damage. All doors disappear at the start of your next turn.

If you chose this as your Innate Talent, you can place two of these doors as a Bonus Action and can connect two of the doors, causing them to become linked. If anything passes through one, it appears out of the other. Linked doors don't deal damage unless you use a Reaction to close it on a person passing through the door.

Counterspell (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

When targeted by any Trait ability, you may choose to use your Reaction to negate the effect. It costs 1 charge to block a 1st level trait, 2 charges for a 6th level trait, 3 for a 13th level, and 4 for a 17th level trait. This has charges equal to your Aura Score per encounter.

Curse Infusion (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action, you can infuse an object with mana, adding an additional 1d6 force damage to the next attack with it and making it have a detectable amount of magic.

Mitigate Damage (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

When you use Health Die to heal, you heal additional Health equal to your Aura score per die spent.

Mystic Aura (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can add an additional force damage equal to your Aura modifier to any unarmed attack or sorcerer Trait. Additionally, your armor is increased by 1. This can be turned on and off as a Bonus Action, but while active you also exude a large amount of magic that can be easily detected even without magic detection.

Plane Shifter (Cost 2):

Type: Sorcerous Talent

As a Bonus Action, you can teleport 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction.

If you chose this as your Innate Talent, you can use this as a Reaction to being attacked, and when you use it as a Bonus Action, if you teleport within 1 meter (3 ft) of another creature, you can immediately make an unarmed attack as a free action. Additionally, rather than just teleporting, you can swap places with another creature in range or swap the places of two creatures within range.

Rough Mana (Cost 2):

Type: Sorcerous Talent

As a Bonus Action, you can fire a projectile within 10 meters (33 ft) that deals 2d6 force damage. Additionally, as a Reaction, you can make a Shield that adds a +2 to your Armor.

If you chose this as your Innate Talent, you use your Main Action to shoot a forceball with a 10 meter radius within 30 meters of you. Everyone in the radius must make a Dexterity check or take 4d8 Explosive damage.

Shadow Manipulation (Cost 2):

Type: Sorcerous Talent

As a Bonus Action, you can make the area around you become shrouded in darkness, giving all creatures within 10 meters (33 ft) other than you a -4 disadvantage on their attacks.

If you chose this as your Innate Talent, during your Movement Actions, you can traverse through the shadows. There must be darkness for you to move through, and if you do move through the shadows, you cannot be attacked and can teleport to other shadows within sight. You are immediately sent out of the shadows at the end of your turn.

Temporal Shift (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per encounter, you can grant yourself and up to 5 allies an additional action. You may still only use one Main Action on your turn.

Third Eye (Cost 2):

Type: Sorcerous Talent

You can see into the Ethereal Plane, allowing you to see ghosts and other spirits. Additionally, any magic user or magic item has a faint glowing aura off of them for you.

Additionally, if you choose this as your Innate Talent, as a Bonus Action, you can reveal all magic that has its aura suppressed from magic detection in a 15 meter (49 ft) radius.

6th Level Traits

Arcane Camouflage (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

You can turn yourself entirely invisible, completely obscuring yourself from vision. You gain the Invisible Status Effect. The effect lasts for 1 minute.

Chant of Empowerment (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

By channeling your inner mana through a rhythmic chant, you can enhance the potency of your abilities. As a Main Action, you can begin chanting to empower a specific Sorcerer Trait you intend to use on your next turn. For each additional Bonus Action spent chanting, the empowered Trait gains increased effects. You can chant for up to six additional actions. You must still use the required action of the Trait upon activation.

Chanting Effects:

  • One Additional Action: The empowered Trait deals an additional 2d6 Magic damage.
  • Two Additional Actions: The empowered Trait deals an additional 3d6 Magic damage.
  • Three Additional Actions: The empowered Trait deals an additional 4d6 Magic damage and the target has disadvantage on saving throws against it.
  • Four Additional Actions: The empowered Trait deals an additional 5d6 Magic damage and the target has disadvantage on saving throws against it.
  • Six Additional Actions: The empowered Trait deals an additional 6d6 Magic damage, the target has disadvantage on saving throws against it, and all creatures within 5 meters (16 ft) of the target take half the damage dealt.

Magic Missile (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action, you can create 3 missiles that deal 1d6 + your Mind die in Magical damage.

Midas Touch (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

When you touch an item, you can transmute its element as a Bonus Action. You can transmute the material to another material of a similar density. You cannot create or destroy material during this process.

Phantom Step (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You become incorporeal for the Movement Action and can move up to half your speed, moving through creatures and solid objects requires twice the speed as moving normally.

Reverberating Strike (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When you deal damage with an unarmed strike, as a Reaction at any point you can cause the target to take the same damage a second time, this time entirely Magical damage.

Simple Domain (Cost 2):

Type: Double Action

You can activate a 12 meter (40 ft) radius Simple Domain as a Double Action. It remains for 30 seconds, and while active all of your Sorcerer Traits ignore Armor against other creatures within the area.

If you have an Innate Talent, you gain the Expanded Domain of its type below. It requires an additional Double Action and can be used Once per Encounter:

  • Arcane Gate: You create an expansive 15 meter (49 ft) radius arcane nexus around you, filled with swirling magical gates. Within this domain, you can summon additional gates, each dealing 4d10 force damage to any creature that passes through. Each creature who moves must roll a Mind check with a Difficulty of 14. If they fail, you decide where they end their turn. Additionally, you can use a Bonus Action to close all gates, trapping any creatures within them and causing them to take an additional 6d10 force damage.
  • Plane Shifter: You create a 20 meter (66 ft) radius celestial field that distorts space and time. Within this domain, you can teleport anywhere in the area as a free action. Enemies within the field must make an Intelligence saving throw or become disoriented, taking 4d10 Magic damage, gaining the Blinded effect, and becoming unable to use any trait abilities for 1 minute.
  • Rough Mana: All your Sorcerer Traits cause creatures to automatically fail their checks against them, and their damage is doubled.
  • Third Eye: You unlock a profound vision that extends to a 30 meter (98 ft) radius around you. Within this area, all hidden and invisible creatures become visible, and all magical auras are revealed. Any creature within the radius must make an Intelligence saving throw or be overwhelmed by visions, granting a -5 disadvantage to all attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks. Additionally, you gain complete knowledge of all active magic effects and can dispel any magic within the area by spending a Reaction.
  • Shadow Manipulation: You enshroud a 20 meter (66 ft) radius area in impenetrable darkness, creating a realm of shadows. All light sources within the domain are extinguished, rendering the area in complete darkness. Enemies within the realm must make a Constitution saving throw or suffer 5d10 Void damage upon activation. Shadowy tendrils lash out from the darkness, dealing 3d10 Void damage to any creature that starts its turn within the domain.

13th Level Traits

Stone Cold Crazy (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You give a creature within sight a +4 advantage against you as a Bonus Action. If that creature hits you, you can automatically take a full attack action targeting that creature without using a Reaction.

Duelist Art: Focus Strike (Cost 2):

Type: Double Action

You must have the Duelist Trait. As a Double Action, you can make a single attack, adding 4d6 + your Mind Score to the attack.

Breaking the Law (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You may use a Bonus Action, and if you do, the next attack you make decreases the target's Armor by 2, whether or not it hits.

Grappler Art: Body Slam (Cost 2):

Type: Free Action

You must have the Grappler Trait. When you use a Jump as your Movement Action, you can choose a creature within the range of your Jump to land on, making an attack for 2d10 + your Power die as a Free Action.

The Last Stand (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

As a Reaction, when you are dropped to 0 Health and have no Health Die, you can return to 1 Health instead and immediately take a turn with a +5 advantage on all rolls during the rest of the round. Can be used once per rest.

Pugilism Art: Grab Break (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

You must have the Pugilism Trait. As a Reaction to being Grabbed, you may immediately make an unarmed attack. If it hits, you are not Grabbed, and the Grabber is unable to use the Grab action for 1 minute or until they heal at least 5 Health.

Ranger Art: Piercing Shot (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You must have the Ranger Trait. When you fire a projectile, you may choose another target within 1 meter (3 ft) to also take the damage. You may then choose another target within 5 feet (5 ft) of that creature that isn't the original target. You may repeat this process until there is no other creature you can hit.

Skirmishing Art: Throwing Stars (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You can throw a thrown weapon as part of a Movement Action.

Special Arts: Heavy Dash (Cost 2):

Type: Movement Action

You must have the Special Arts Trait. You can move up to half your Movement as a Movement Action, and any creature within 1 meter (3 ft) of the path you took takes Explosive damage equal to your Dexterity die + your Power die. You can Grab a creature within 1 meter (3 ft) at the end of your Movement if able.

Weapon Mastery (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You must have the Weapon Proficiency Trait. You gain a +2 to attacks with all weapons.

17th Level Traits

Grappler Art: Boundless Piledriver (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Double Action, you can perform the Boundless Piledriver on a creature you have Grabbed, a creature within 1 meter (3 ft) around you, or 3 meters (9 ft) into the air above you. You then deal 10d10 + five of your Power Die in Explosive damage and jump 10 meters (33 ft) into the air. You can choose to drop the creature there or land with them. You don't take fall damage either way.

Special Arts: Augmented Power (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

As a Bonus Action, Once per Encounter, you can choose one of the following Augmented Power styles, which remain for 30 seconds:

  • Power of Absolute Offense: Add 1d8 Explosive damage to your attacks, and you can make one additional attack when you use the attack action.
  • Power of Undying Defense: Add a +2 to your Armor, you can't be moved against your will, and when you take damage that would drop you to 0 Health, you stay at 1 Health until this ends.
  • Power of True Speed: Your movement speed is tripled, and you no longer need to target only one creature when you use Focused Flurry.

True Power (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per Encounter, you can choose for an attack to ignore Armor and Resistances. It is automatically a Critical Attack.
