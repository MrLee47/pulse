1st Level Traits

Fireball (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action (Cooldown: None)

You can make a 2d4 Explosive damage attack against someone within 3 meters (16 ft) as a Bonus Action. As a Double Action you may choose to amp the attack to a single 4d6 beam that hits everyone in 1 x 5 meter *3 x 16 feet) line forward. This Double Action has a 5 round cooldown.

Flash Kick (Cost 2):

Type: Movement Action (Cooldown: 3 Rounds)

As a Bonus Action you can prepare a Flash Kick. When a creature is about to hit you with an attack you can use Flash Kick without a Reaction, dealing 3d4 Physical damage and ignoring Armor. If their attack would connect, you take halved damage.

Footdive (Cost 1):

Type: Free Action (Cooldown: None)

When you end a Movement Action in the air you may Footdive as a Free Action, and there is no limit to the amount of times you can do this in a turn. The attack is 2d8 Physical damage. If you don't hit, the target still takes 1d4 Explosive damage.

Tiger Knee (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action (Cooldown: 3 Rounds):

Using your Main Action you can choose a target and fly through the air towards them, up to your Speed, if you end this within range you can deal either 2d4 + Power Score Physical damage, or use your weapon to attack once you reach.

Lightning Wind God Fist (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action (Cooldown: 5 Rounds)

You make a regular attack with your weapon, if it hits, the opponent takes an additional 1d4 Explosive damage and are knocked 5 ft into the air. You then gain another Bonus Action. Also as a Bonus Action, you can make an attack against a creature in the air. If it hits, you knock the creature down, the cool down for Lightning Wind God Fist decreases by 5 rounds, and you gain another Bonus Action. If you miss, the cooldown for Lightning Wind God Fist increases by 2 rounds.

10th Level Traits

Dragon Install (Cost 3):

Type: Once per Encounter (Cooldown: 3 Encounters)

You become infused with firey energy, dealing an extra 1d8 Explosive damage with all attacks, tripling your Speed and Jump distance, and increasing your Armor by 2. It lasts for the rest of the encounter.

Falcon Punch (Cost 2):

Main Action (Cooldown: 10 Rounds)

You punch someone. This deals 10d10 Explosive damage + Power Score in Physical damage.

Raging Demon (Cost 4):

Type: Double Action (Cooldown 10 rounds):

You give the Frozen effect to a target within range. Your speed is quintupled for the rest of this turn, and you must make 10 attacks against that target. You gain a +1 to melee attacks for 5 rounds afterwards.
