Hunter

Home Classes Origins Items Prestige

Health

Health per Level: 6 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d8 per Level

Sharpshooter:

When you choose this class once per turn as a Free Action you can roll an Insight check against a targets Armor, and if you pass all attacks against them have a +4 advantage. Additionally, when using a Firearm or Bow, attacking doesn't require any form of action, you can use your shots at any point during your turn or after your turn as a sort of reaction. You gain an additional shot now, second at 6th, a third at 12th, and a fourth at 18.

Phantom Step:

When a creature attacks you, you can use a Reaction to move up to your speed. If this moves you out of the attacks range, it causes it to miss. If it doesn't, the attack has a -5 disadvantage. If using this feature causes an attack to miss, you regain your reaction.

1st Level Traits

Jawbreaker (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

Once per turn you can expend a Bonus Action to make an unarmed strike. If it hits, the target is Stunned until the end of your turn.

Bullrush (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

You move up to your speed, if you end your turn within 2 meters (6 ft) of someone, you have a +2 advantage and immediately attack.

Armored Hammer (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You can use a Bonus Action to move up to 10 meters (33 ft) and if you're attacked during this movement you can add 1d10 Explosive damage to your next attack this turn.

Very Ape (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you use the Toss free action after a grab, you deal Physical damage equal to your Power score.

Juggernaut (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You cannot be moved or get Knocked against your will.

Menace (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

After you make an attack that kills or drops a creature to 0 health, you can choose someone within 10 meters (33 feet) to roll an Insight check against your Aura score. If they fail, they gain the Fear condition until the end of their next turn.

Overpower (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

When you make an attack you can choose to add a +5 to attack, and if the attack hits you knockdown the target.

Relentless Pursuit (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You can move through the space of other creatures.

Rock Solid (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

When a non-magical weapon hits you with a critical miss it cracks, causing it to has a Disadvantage of -2. If it gets to -10 the weapon breaks.

Thick Skin (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You gain 1 health now, and an additional 1 health each level.

6th Level Traits

Brutal Taunt (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action you can cause all creatures within 10 meters (33 ft) of you to have a -3 disadvantage on attacks against people other than you.

Bulwark of Fear (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When a creature misses you with an attack they must roll a Insight check against your Aura score or gain the Fear condition.

Controlled Panic (Cost 1):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action you can choose a creature with the Fear effect and force it to move up to half your movement speed in a direction of your choice.

Overbearing Advance (Cost 1):

Type: Movement Action

You can move up to 5 meters (16 ft) ignoring attacks, and if you come in contact with another creature they take 1d8 + Power die in Physical damage. This ignores Armor.

Punishing Grasp (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When a creature within 1 meter (3 ft) of you successfully attacks you, you can immediately Grab them as a Reaction.

Shatter (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

As your Main Action you can force everyone within 10 meters (33 ft) of you to become Knocked Additionally each creature gets attacked for 2d8 Physical damage.

Rebel Yell (Cost 1):

Type: Main Action

For the next 30 seconds all creatures of your choice within 10 meters (33 feet) have disadvantage on Insight checks.

13th Level Traits

Focused Fury (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

You form a massive ball of energy in your hand as your Main Action. Any creature with 3 meters (9 ft) of you take 4d10 + your Aura die in Explosive damage. Additionally, if they have the Fear effect you can add your Power Score.

Meteoric Impact (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

Choose a creature within 10 meters (33 ft). Using your Main Action you can launch yourself towards them attacking for 4d6 + your Power die in Explosive damage. If the creature has the Fear effect you can add your Power Score to the attack.

Siesmic Stomp (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You can Jump as a Bonus Action, then when you land all creatures within 10 ft are attacked for 2d4 Physical damage.

Devastating Dark Destructive Deadly Cleave (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

Make a melee attack against a creature within 10 ft. They take 2d10 Explosive and 1d10 Void damage. Any creature within 2 meters of that creature also takes the damage, and then within 2 meters of those creatures, etcetera. It does not target you, and can never hit the same creature twice.

Wrecking Ball (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you use a melee attack against a construct or solid material, and roll the max on damage, the construct is destroyed.

17th Level Traits

Cataclysmic Strike (Cost 4):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per Encounter you can replace an attack with a Punt. This does your normal attack damage, but knocks the target 3 meters (9 ft) into the air. It remains there and gains the Stunned effect for 10 seconds if the attack hits. At any point while the creature is Stunned, without using an action or Reaction, you can then hit it with a Cataclysmic Strike. It deals 8d10 Physical damage, and knocks the creature 5 kilometers (1640 ft) into the air in a direction of your choice.

Color of the Supreme King (Cost 4):

Type: Bonus Action

As a Bonus Action you can choose any number of creatures who have the Fear effect inflicted on them and Stun them for 20 seconds. You can also use this as a Reaction when a creature within sight gains the Fear effect. You cannot use this on a creature who has the Stunned effect already.

Walk (Cost 3):

Type: Main Action

Choose a creature within sight as your Main Action. The creature must have the Fear effect. That creature must immediately spend both actions on its next turn to run as far as it possibly can from you.
