Hunter

Home Classes Origins Items Prestige

Health

Health per Level: 6 + Constitution

Health Die: 1d8 per Level

Sharpshooter:

When you choose this class once per turn as a Free Action you can roll an Insight check against a targets Armor, and if you pass all attacks of your against them have a +4 advantage against them until the start of your next turn. Additionally, when using a Firearm or Bow, attacking doesn't require any form of action, you can use your shots at any point during your turn or after your turn as a sort of reaction. You gain an additional shot now, second at 6th, a third at 12th, and a fourth at 18.

Phantom Step:

When a creature attacks you, you can use a Reaction to move up to your speed. If this moves you out of the attacks range, it causes it to miss. If it doesn't, the attack has a -5 disadvantage. If using this feature causes an attack to miss, you regain your reaction.

1st Level Traits

Drive Strike (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

You can use a reaction to make an unarmed strike when a creature enters your range. If it hits, the target is knocked back 5 meters (16 ft). If it misses, the target is knocked by 1 meter (3 ft). You can use this as a Main Action if there is a creature in your range on your turn.

Drop Shot (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

When you make an attack with a Firearm or Bow you can give yourself a -2 disadvantage, if it hits the target roll a Sleight of Hand check against the hit, or becomes disarmed.

Fall Back (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you miss an attack you can do a barrel roll 5 meters (16 ft) in any direction. You can Disengage as a Free Action once per turn.

Fan the Hammer (Cost 2):

Type: Bonus Action

You make one attack with a Bow or Firearm as a Bonus Action. If you use this and have the Sharpshooter feature you add 2 more shots to your total allowed this round.

Hunter's Eye (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

You have a +2 advantage on all Insight and Perception checks.

Pinning Shot (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

If you make an attack against a target that is within 3 meters (9 ft) of a wall or object, you can force it to roll a Constitution check against your roll, and on a fail you push it up against that object and cause them to be pinned to the wall and unable to move until they succeed an Athletics check against the weapons roll to hit when they try to move on their turn. If you shoot multiple Pinning Shots they have to succeed all of the checks to be able to move. If they have more than one they are also unable to attack.

Richochet Round (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you make an attack with a Firearm or Bow, you can delcare a Richochet shot, and choose where to shoot it. Roll to hit, and it will bounce to the target it is closest to being able to hit, or whichever it is able to hit. If it can hit both, the Game Master must tell you, and you can choose which to target.

Skullrocker (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you roll a Critical Hit the target has a -4 disadvantage on Mind checks until your next turn.

Slow Down (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

hen you make an attack with a Firearm or Bow you can give yourself a -2 disadvantage, if it hits the targets speed is quartered.

6th Level Traits

Bleeding Cool (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you make an attack with a Firearm or Bow you can give yourself a -2 disadvantage, if it hits you apply a stack of Bleed.

Callouts (Cost 1):

Type: Reaction

When an ally makes an attack against a creature you can use your Reaction to give them a +3 advantage. Alternatively you can prepare this as a Bonus Action, and activate it at any point before your next turn without using your Reaction.

Confusing Shot (Cost 1):

Type: Passive

When you make an attack with a Firearm or Bow you can give yourself a -2 disadvantage, if it hits the target has a -4 disadvantage on their current or next attack or check.

Critical Reflex (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

When you roll a Critical Hit, you gain an additional Reaction.

Dazing Shot (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

When you make an attack with a Firearm or Bow you can give yourself a -2 disadvantage, if it hits you can force the target to roll a Constitution check against the hit, and on a failure it applies the Stun effect to the target until the start of your next turn.

Quick Maneuvers (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

Your Speed increases by 5 meters. Additionally if you have Phantom Step, whether or not the attack misses after its resolved you can take a Movement Action for free.

Quickshot (Cost 2):

Type: Passive

You can make an attack with a Bow or Firearm as part of a Movement Action.

13th Level Traits

Aim Down (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

Any attack that has advantage on it until your next turn has its advantage doubled.

Desperado's Gambit (Cost 1):

Type: Once per Encounter

You use a Double Action and choose up to 10 creatures, and you have your Critical Hit range tripled. Make an attack against each creature. You cannot use Phantom Step or Sharpshooter until the start of your next turn.

Punisher's End (Cost 2):

Type: Once per Encounter

Once per encounter you can choose to activate Punisher's End when you make an attack targeting a creature, and then no matter what is currently happening, up to 5 allies of your choice immediately uses their main action to make an attack targeting the creature.

Swift Vengeance (Cost 2):

Type: Reaction

When a creature hits you with an attack, you can immediately use a Reaction to make an attack against them using a Bow or Firearm. If you have the Phantom Step feature and this attack hits, you regain your Reaction.

Target Lock (Cost 2):

Type: Main Action

You can choose a target within sight and roll a Perception check against their Armor, if you succeed you cannot have disadvantage imposed on your attacks against that creature through any means. You use up to two traits that impose disadvantage on yourself before you must start calculating the disadvantage.

17th Level Traits

Inhuman Accuracy (Cost 4):

Type: Passive

You can add your Mind and Dexterity score to attacks with Firearms or Bows.

Not An Angel (Cost 4):

Type: Double Reaction

As a reaction to an attack targeting you or an ally, you can use two reactions, as time seems to slow down. You completely dodge all attacks targeting you for 20 seconds and anytime someone targets you with an attack during this time you can immediately make an attack back without a Reaction. If an ally within 10 meters (33 ft) is targeted during this time you can move to the attacker and stop them from attacking as a Reaction.

Hurricane of Steel (Cost 4):

Type: Once Per Encounter

As a Main Action if you have the Sharpshooter ability you can double the number of shots you can make for 20 seconds. Additionally you can take an additional Movement Action on each of your turns during this time.
